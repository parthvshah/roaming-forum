About Us

The purpose, the vision.

This project was developed for UE17CS203, Web Technologies for PES University. This is our project. We would like to thank Prof. Oshin Sharma for her guidance.

The concept of a forum with rights to post on any topic, by anyone was born as a reaction to recent censorship which has become excessive of late. We promote free speech in the real sense of it and people love it.

This website uses HTML with Bootstrap for this amazing look and feel. All images are copyright Parth Shah. The front-end scripting is in JavaScript and the back-end is written in PHP. The database is MySQL.

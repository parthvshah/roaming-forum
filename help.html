Help

This section has all the information you need to operate on our website. It's extremely easy!

Head over to the create page and type in your article. Provide all the details mentioned in the form. Once you submit, head over to the main page to see your post! You or other users can now upvote, downvote, comment on posts. The number of upvotes is in green, the number of downvotes is in red. To vote, click on one of the corresponding arrows.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an article?

You can add an article from the create page. The navigation to this page can be found at the top of every page. Type in your article and add relavent details. This text is then published on our blog!

How do I view the articles on this blog?

Head over to the home page of this blog to see all the articles published on this blog. Feel free to react to any of the articles via the comment section or upvoting/downvoting. To contribute, you can go to the 'Create' page.

How do I upvote/downvote or comment?

On any article of your interest, click on the green arrow to upvote, the red arrow to downvote or comment using the text input. Make sure you keep your interactions friendly and constructive.

How do I contact the developers of this awesome website?

Head over to the 'Contact Us' page and submit your details and any query you may have. We shall get in touch with you as soon as we can!
